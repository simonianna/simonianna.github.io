About

I am a senior researcher at the CNRS affiliated with CREST and Professor of Statistics and Econometrics at ENSAE and École Polytechnique.

I hold a PhD and HDR from the Toulouse School of Economics.

  • Associate Editor of the Journal of Econometrics
  • Fellow of Hi! Paris
  • Fellow of the Institut Louis Bachelier
  • Member of the Economic Data Science Society
